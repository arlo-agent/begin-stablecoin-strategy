### Typography
- **Balance:** Large, bold, confident (SF Pro Display or Inter)
- **Section headers:** ALL CAPS, small, muted
- **Body:** Clean, readable, good contrast
- **Numbers:** Tabular figures (aligned decimals)

### Iconography
- Rounded, friendly icons
- Consistent stroke weight
- Token logos: circular, high-res
- Chain indicators: subtle, only when relevant
